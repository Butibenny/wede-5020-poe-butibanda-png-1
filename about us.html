OUR HISTORY:
Jimmy Neutron Electricians was established in 2019 by Lucky Jimmy Ndlovu. After experiencing the daily disruptions caused by loadshedding, and using his qualification in electrical engineering, he founded the company to provide reliable electrical solutions. We believe electricians are the invisible backbone of modern progress without electricity, most of daily life stops. Our mission is to always be available for people who need qualified electricians. We combine time-honoured craftsmanship, established by the first master wiremen, with 21st-century smart technology to power the next generation of homes and businesses. Our services cater to both residential and commercial clients who need professional, trustworthy electrical work. Our mission and vision:
