Safety Tips

Follow these important safety guidelines to protect yourself and others.

At Home

Keep emergency numbers near your phone.
Install smoke detectors and check plugs regularly.
Store electrical components out of reach of children.
Always protect electrical staff.
Do not use your wet hands on cables.
Follow electricity rules.

Online Safety

Use strong, unique passwords for each account.
Always verify you are in the correct website.
Be cautious when clicking on book.
